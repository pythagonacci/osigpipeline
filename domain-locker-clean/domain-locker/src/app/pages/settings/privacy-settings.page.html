Privacy Settings

With Domain Locker, you are in control of your data, and how it's used.

Hit Counting

This enables us to see approximate number of active sessions on Domain Locker, and helps us ensure we're provisioning enough resources to provide a smooth experience to everyone.

We use a self-hosted instance of Plausible. No user data or PII is collected or stored.

Error Tracking

This sends us a stack trace when an error occurs, to help us identify and fix bugs.

We use a self-hosted instance of GlitchTip. No sensitive data should be collected or stored

Performance Monitoring

Recoding of key performance metrics, such as page load times and resource usage. This helps us identify and fix performance bottlenecks, in order to help us develop a better user experience.

We use a self-hosted instance of Uptrace. No PII is collected or stored

Cookies

We don't use any 1st or 3rd-party cookies to track or store any of your data.

Local Storage

We use local storage to store your local preferences and settings, as well as managing session timeout.

It's not possible to disable this, as it's required for the app to function. But you can clear this data at any time.

Account

View our Privacy page for more information on what data is collected, how it's stored and what it's used for. You can export your data and/or delete your account (and all associated data) at any time.
